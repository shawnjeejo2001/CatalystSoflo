Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

A typical website takes about 3-4 weeks from start to finish. More complex projects can take 5-8 weeks. The timeline always depends on the project's scope and how quickly we receive content and feedback from you.

What do you need from me to get started?

To start, we just need to have a discovery call to understand your goals. Once we begin, you'll need to provide any existing branding (like your logo), text content for the pages (we can help with this), and any specific images you want to use.

Will I be able to update the website myself?

Your website is custom-coded for the best possible performance, security, and SEO. While this means updates require a developer, we handle them for you quickly and efficiently. For clients who anticipate regular updates, we offer 'Website Warranty' packages that make this process seamless.

What are the ongoing costs after the site is built?

As we outline in our "Our Approach" section, we don't charge mandatory monthly fees. The only recurring costs you will have are for your domain name (usually about $15/year) and website hosting (which can range from $10-$30/month depending on the provider you choose). We can help you get this set up.
